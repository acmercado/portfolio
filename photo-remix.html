title:

ignorance

date:

02/07/2018

medium:

.jpgs, .pngs, compiled and found online.

description:

based on the fact that we, as humans, have been destroying the natural life that once used to surround us and act 'ignorant' to the truth for the living styles that we created for ourselves.

process:

i used different pictures that i pulled from Google Images and used the "masking" tool in PhotoShop. i also played with the image saturation settings to make the pictures "fit" each other more. this was my first time using PhotoShop, so i hope to improve my skills in the near future.
